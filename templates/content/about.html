President, Chief Executive Officer

Beth Mayes

Beth has spent the majority of her professional career in the publishing industry and has extensive experience innovating platforms that host digital media. As the Director of Development for Springer platforms, she coordinated the efforts of architects, developers, societies, editorial boards, libraries, marketers and licensing managers for one of the largest scholarly content platforms in the world.

Focusing on the rapid migration to digital media and constant technological advancement, Beth endeavors to seamlessly connect the myriad systems required for publishing, hosting, discovery and delivery of online content.

Beth graduated from Texas A&M University and held earlier positions in education, customer support, marketing, sales and product management.

President, Chief Technical Officer

Joshua Pyle

Josh has over 15 years of experience in the publishing industry and was the co-founder of MetaPress, a division of EBSCO. As Chief Technical Officer and Chief Architect he led the business to become the world's largest primary scholarly content host. He also managed the development and operations for nearly 200 publisher clients.

In recent years Josh has become passionate about massively-scalable, highly-available, robust, concurrent systems. With the increasing demand of users and performance expectations of hosting platforms, he is anxious to offer innovative, intuitive alternatives to publishers and libraries.

Josh graduated from Virginia Tech and held earlier positions as Content Development Manager for EBSCO Online and as an automation engineer at Trane.
